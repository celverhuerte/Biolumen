About

BIOLUMEN comes from the Greek word "bios" and the Latin word "lumen" which means "lifelight". It is a website orignally created to be a submission for the Future Ready Asean Competition 2019. It was created by Racquel Alyssa F. Catolico and Filbert Joseph A. Ubanos, two scholars from Philippine Science High School - Main Campus. This website aims to shed light on the problems that plague the biodiversity of the world and the ASEAN Region specifically, in hopes of becoming a platform for people to know more and act.
